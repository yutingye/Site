I am an R&D engineer at Industrial Light & Magic.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Graphics Group and the GVU Center of Georgia Institute of Technology under the advice of Dr. C. Karen Liu. I'm interested in physically-based simulation and motion control of human-like behaviors for 3D virtual characters. I'm currently looking for a research position in animation or robotics related areas. My past experiences are summarized in this CV.

I received a M.CS. degree from the University of Virginia in 2006, where I worked with Dr. David C. Brogan in the graphics group on a momentum-based bipedal controller. I then spent a year in the University of Southern California with Karen before we moved to Gatech. I received a B.S. degree in Computer Science from Peking University in Bejing, China in 2004.
